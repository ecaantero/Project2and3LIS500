Implicit bias This is a placeholder Implicit bias is an automatic, unconscious attitude or stereotype that shapes how we think about and treat others. It’s a quick conclusion your brain makes without you even realizing it. These biases often develop from what we absorb through culture, media, family, and personal experiences. Even if we consciously reject certain stereotypes, implicit biases can still influence our decisions and behavior without our awareness.
