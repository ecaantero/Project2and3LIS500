Tech Hero

My tech is Moses West, the founder of AWG Contracting. He is the engineer behind a solar powered water generator that provides clean drinking water in disaster stricken and underserved communities. His technology continued to supply nearly 400 gallons of water daily in Accompong, Jamaica, even after Hurricane Melissa destroyed local infrastructure. West focuses on delivering safe, renewable, off-grid water solutions to communities affected by water insecurity, including Flint, Jackson, and Puerto Rico. He is now scaling production in the U.S. through a $25 million clean-manufacturing initiative to expand global access to climate-resilient water technology.
